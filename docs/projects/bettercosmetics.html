Better Cosmetics Documentation

Better Cosmetics

Better Cosmetics adds a greater level of character customization to ARK: Survival Ascended! Utilize our advanced configuration UI to change your skin style, color (utilizing a color-picker capable of providing any color you desite!), birthmarks, and scars. Add certain 'parts' to your character (please note, these are currently in-development and only a small number are available) as well as 'cosmetics' such as hats, glasses, and removal of the specimen implant.

Current Version: Beta v0.3.0

INI Settings

To see all available INI options, please head over to: INI Settings

Skin Customization

  • Skin Styles: Several skin styles are available, including the basic human skin and a cat-like fur skin!
  • Skin Coloring: Utilizing a full color picker, you have full control over your character's skin color. Reds, greens, blues, you name it and you can make your character can have it.
  • Eye Coloring: Choose custom colors for both inner and outer eye color, making use of a fully fledged color wheel!
  • Markings: From stripes to scars and birthmarks, you're able to choose up to 5 skin markings to further diversify your character!

    • Parts Customization

  • Add various 'parts' to your character!
  • Supports rotation, scaling, and repositioning of all parts!
  • Please note: While Better Cosmetics is in it's Beta, only a very limited number of parts are available.

    • Cosmetics Customization

  • Jewlery: Base-game glasses models are available for your use!
  • Hats: Several base-game hats are available for you to add to your character, permanently!
  • & More: Removal of the specimen implant from your arm!
  • Full Customiation: Move, rotate, and rescale your cosmetics to fully suit your character!

    • Hair Customization

  • Head & Facial Hair Styles: Select any style from base-ARK, or any mod that adds hair. Customize the length of your hair tips, from non-existent to 2x what base-ARK allows!
  • Eyebrow Styles: Select any style from base-ARK, or any mod that adds eyebrow styles!
  • Colorization: Utilize the full color picker wheel to color your hair and eyebrows any color of the rainbow, and more!
  • Note: In order to maintain compatibility with other mods, some hair colors may show better than others.

    • Saving Your Customizations

  • Local Saves: Save your character's skin type, color, markings, parts/cosmetics, and hair selection locally to your computer. You can load this template at any time, on any gender character, from any server.
  • Save path: 
    ShooterGame/Saved/SaveGames/BetterCosmetics/LocalData/
  • Templates: Create an item with a custom name, description, and icon (colorization options available!). Useful for quickly switching appearances on a single server. Note: These templates are non-transferrable cross-servers.

    • Commissions

    The following items may be commissioned by joining our Discord:

  • Skin Markings (ie: birthmarks, scars)
  • Parts
  • Cosmetic Accessories
