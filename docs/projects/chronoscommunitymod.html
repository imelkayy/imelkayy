Chronos Community Mod

The Chronos Community Mod is designed specifically for the Chronos server. Support will not be given out for communities or individuals other than Chronos.

Features

  • Custom-built decay functionality replacing the vanilla decay system.
  • Handles decay over a period of days, when a structure is not within range of a decay preventor.
  • Decay preventors will decay after a tribe has not been online for a period of time, then triggering decay for all structures it was protecting.
