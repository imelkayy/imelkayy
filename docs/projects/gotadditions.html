GoT Additions Documentation

GoT Additions

GoTAdditions adds a variety of Game of Thrones inspired items to ARK: Survival Ascended! Shield skins, with four unique styles, for various Houses from the Game of Thrones universe can be crafted in the Smithy. House banners are also coming soon! This mod is fully crossplatform, and compatible with all platforms (PC, Xbox Series X and S, PS5).

Shields

Styles: Four beautiful shield styles are available: Kite, Curved Kite, Round, and Tall

Variants: Shield styles for various houses are available, including: Greyjoy, Lannister, Stark, Targaryen, Tyrell, and Velaryon

Skins: All shields are skins, craftable inside a smithy (without the need for engrams!) and cost 1 fiber each.

Efficiency: Shield styles make use of only a single material and optimized textures, ensuring as minimal a file size as possible! Please note: if you leave a shield equipped after logging out, you will need to reequip it for the texture to reapply upon login.
