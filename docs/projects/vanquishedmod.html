Vanquished Gaming Community Mod Documentation

Vanquished Gaming Community Mod

The Vanquished Gaming Community Mod is designed for the Vanquished Gaming community server. Support will not be given out for communities or individuals other than Vanquished.

Features

  • Includes a King of the Hill minigame system. Admins must place a Flag Location in order for the system to be enabled, and flag spawns take between 10 and 30 minutes (by default).
  • Adds the Vanquished Gaming logo as an overlay in the bottom left.
